BOATHOUSE

Our bay is located at Three Rivers Rowing Association in Washington's Landing, Pittsburgh. We row primarily on the Allegheny River, but occasionally our practices take us on the Monongahela and Ohio Rivers. We have more than 21k of rowable water stretching from Sharpsburg all the way down to Homestead.

FLEET

Our team primarily sweeps, housing seven 8+ shells and six 4+ shells. However, we also maintain a small fleet of sculling boats, with six 2x/- shells, and two 1x shells. Our shells are sourced from a variety of manufacturers, ranging from Hudson, Empacher, Vespoli, King, and Wintech.

INDOOR FACILITY

Our indoor training facility is located in Bellefield Hall, a five minute walk from the Cathedral of Learning. In addition to our private erg room, there is access to a weight room and pool downstairs.
